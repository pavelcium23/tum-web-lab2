Questions

Frequently Asked

Do you 3D print your props?▾
No. Every build is entirely handmade — foam cut and shaped manually, composites layered by hand. No 3D-printed components, ever.
How does the build process work?▾
It starts with a consultation to align on character, references, and scale. I then take your measurements, build a full 3D digital model to verify proportions, and only then start the physical build — foam shaping, reinforcement, surface finishing, and paint work. Photos are sent for approval before delivery.
Can you scale a prop to my body size?▾
Yes — this is a core part of the process. Dimensions are adjusted to your measurements specifically, not the in-game or reference proportions.
How far in advance should I reach out?▾
It depends on complexity and current queue. Sooner is always better — include your event date so I can advise on availability.
Do you ship internationally?▾
Yes, depending on project size — with secure packaging and tracking. Include your country when inquiring.
How is pricing determined?▾
Every commission is custom. Pricing depends on size, complexity, detail level, and deadline. Submit an inquiry and I'll respond with a quote.
